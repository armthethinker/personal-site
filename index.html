Moral Decisions & Haptics in VR 🚋

An exploration of the trolley problem

Moral questions are often posed in writing or conversation. We created a virtual environment to test an isomorph of the Trolley Problem. Using VR, we were able to situate participants in a realistic scenario whereas traditional thought experiments are tested through text or conversation. We found that participants who have haptic feedback are less likely to kill one person — in order to save four — than their non-haptic counterparts.
